Project Overview

Title: Lily's Journey to Happiness
Genre: Animated Short Film
Duration: Approximately 5 minutes
Theme: Exploration of self-discovery, resilience, and the true meaning of happiness.
Target Audience: All ages

Plot Synopsis

Embark on an enchanting adventure with Lily, a determined little penguin, as it sets out on a quest to find the elusive Happy Land. Guided by a mysterious stranger and fueled by the love and wisdom of its parents, Lily encounters challenges, storms, and moments of doubt. In a heartwarming twist, Lily discovers that true happiness isn't a distant destination but a journey within oneself. This animated short film explores the resilience, self-discovery, and enduring joy found in the pursuit of happiness, reminding us that sometimes, the most magical places are within our own hearts.
